How to Play Go

The Rules of Go

Go is played between two players, Black and White, who alternate turns and attempt to surround the most empty points (spaces): scoring one point per surrounded point at the end of the game. If a group (orthogonally contiguous monotone string of stones) has no liberties (adjacent empty points) at the end of a player's turn, it is captured by the opponent and will score one point per captured stone for him at the end of the game. There are a few additional rules which cover edge cases, but the above is the basis of the game.
